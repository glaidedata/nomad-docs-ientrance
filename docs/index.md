# How to Upload and Share Your Sample Files

Welcome to the iEntrance NOMAD Oasis!

Follow these steps to securely upload your data, attach the necessary context, and share it with our review team.

### Step 1: Go to your Uploads
Log in to the NOMAD Oasis. In the main menu on the left, click on **PUBLISH > Uploads**.

### Step 2: Create a New Upload
Click the **CREATE A NEW UPLOAD** button. At the top of the new page, type a descriptive name for your upload (e.g., *"SEM Raw Data - Project Alpha"*) so our team knows what it is.

### Step 3: Add Your Files
Simply drag and drop your sample files directly into the blue upload box on the screen, or click the box to browse your computer and select them. *(Note: You can upload any type of file here!)*

### Step 4: Add an Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN)
To help us properly integrate your data, please include an ELN entry to provide essential context.

1. Click the **Create from schema** button. 
2. In the built-in schema dropdown, select **Basic ELN**.
3. Give it a name and press **CREATE**. 
4. In the ELN form that appears, please fill out the **Description** field with specific details about your data, including:
    * Your name and email address (so we can contact you if we need more info).
    * The instrument maker and model.
    * The type of measurement performed.
    * Any other relevant context about the file format.
5. Press the **Floppy Disk** icon on the top-right to save the entry.

### Step 5: Share the Data
To let us review your files, look for the **Edit upload members** section on the top right side of the page (look for the human icon). 

1. In the drop-down, select **Group**.
2. In the search bar, type exactly: **Test Data Review**.
3. Select our group, set the role to **Reviewer**, and click submit. 

### Step 6: You are done! 
Your data is now safely stored in your private staging area and shared with our team for review. **You do not need to click "Publish"!**
