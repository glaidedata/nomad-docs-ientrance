# How to Upload and Share Your Sample Files

Welcome to the iEntrance NOMAD Oasis! To help our team efficiently process and support your specific data formats, please follow this step-by-step guide to securely upload your files, attach the necessary context, and share them with our reviewers.

---

### Step 1: Go to your Uploads
Log in to the NOMAD Oasis. In the main menu on the left side of your screen, navigate to **PUBLISH** > **Uploads**.

### Step 2: Create a New Upload
Click the **CREATE A NEW UPLOAD** button. At the top of the new page, type a descriptive name for your upload so our team can easily identify it. 
> *Example: "SEM Raw Data - Project Alpha"*

### Step 3: Add Your Files
You can easily add your files by dragging and dropping them directly into the **blue upload box** on the screen. Alternatively, click the box to browse your computer and select the files manually. 

!!! note "Supported Files"
    You can upload **any** type of file here (e.g., `.txt`, `.csv`, `.rasx`, `.ibw`, proprietary raw files, etc.)!

### Step 4: Add an Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN)
*This is a crucial step!* To help us properly integrate and parse your data, we need some essential context.

1. Click the **Create from schema** button (located near the upload box).
2. In the built-in schema dropdown menu, select **Basic ELN**.
3. Give your ELN a recognizable name and press **CREATE**.
4. In the ELN form that appears, please fill out the **Description** field with specific details about your data.

!!! info "Required Details for the Description"
    Please make sure to include the following in your description:
    
    * **Your Name and Email:** So we can quickly contact you if we have questions about the format.
    * **Instrument Details:** The maker and model of the instrument used.
    * **Measurement Type:** The specific type of measurement performed.
    * **Additional Context:** Any other relevant information about the experiment or the file itself.

5. When you are finished, press the **Floppy Disk** icon (💾) in the top-right corner to save your entry.

### Step 5: Share the Data
To allow our development team to review your files, you need to grant us access. Look for the **Edit upload members** section on the top right side of the page (indicated by the human icon).

1. In the drop-down menu, change the selection to **Group**.
2. In the search bar, type exactly: **`Test Data Review`**
3. Select our group, set the role to **Reviewer**, and click **Submit**.

### Step 6: You are Done! 🎉
Your data is now safely stored in your private staging area and successfully shared with our team for review. 

!!! warning "Do Not Publish"
    **You do not need to click "Publish"!** Leaving it in your staging area allows us to freely review and test parsing on your files without permanently locking the dataset.
